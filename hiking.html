Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone is my favotite national park for a number of reasons. Yellowstone was the first national park that I went to. It was very special for me to be explroing in the Wyoming mountains. Also, it was fun to see Old Faithful in person. I also experienced many great hikes around the park, and enjoyed the time with my family. Lastly, I loved all of the wildlife. I saw herds of bison, mooses, and more. I even spotted a wolf one when driving back from a hike in the evening. It was very cool to see the wolf appear in the trees on the side of the road, and how it acted. Yellowstone will always be a very special place to me!

Favorite Hikes and Experiences

I think that each national park is incredibly special and unoique. When I hike in a different park, the experiece is different each time, but still amazing. For instance, when I was in Zion National Park, it was 100 degrees Farenheight every day. However, when I was in Glacier National Park, it was actively snowing at the top of a big peak in July!. In each park, I can alawyas experience something new!
